Hillary Diane Clinton

Hillary's Website

Hillary Diane Clinton was born on October 26,1947. She was grew up in Park Ridge, Illinois. Hillary graduated school at Maine South High School.

Then, she went onto graduate from Wellesley College. After college, she attended Yale Law School. She married Bill Clinton and had one child, Chelsea Clinton.

Hillary was a Republican until 1968, when she meet Bill. From January 1982 to December 1992, she was the first lady of Arkansas.

Her husband, Bill Clinton, ran for president as a democrat, and Hillary became the first lady of the United Sates from 2001 to 2009.

Hillary Clinton is the Democratic candidate for the 2016 presidential elections.
