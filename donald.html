Donald John Trump was born on June 14, 1946. He was grew up in Queens, New York. Donald attended school at The Kew-Forest School and graduated from the New York Military Academy.

Then, he went onto graduate from University of Pennsylvania. He married Melania Trump and had five child, Ivanka, Barron, Tiffany, Eric, and Donald Trump Jr.

Though Trump does not have a lot of background in politics, he is a very good businessman. He has owned and operated businesses all over, and he knows

how to take down his opponent. Donald Trump is the Republican candidate for the 2016 Presidential Elections.
